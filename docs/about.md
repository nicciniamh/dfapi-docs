# About the Author

 My name is Nicole Stevens. I am a retired software engineer have spent my life working with industrial control systems. I started my professional career working at Tymnet in the mid-1980s. I have basically spent my life making machines talk to one another.

I have over 40 years using, maintaining or coding for \*nix systems.

I like to think I bring a unique perspective to systems management bringing ideas from industrial controls and system administration and typing them together.

## Types of Projects

* Communications Networks
* Industrial Water Purification
* Warehouse Automation
* Medical Systems
* Avionics
* Data Center Management
